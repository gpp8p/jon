Driven By The Past
A Road Trip Through the American West...
Since 2016, I have driven some 65,000 miles and visited over 300 places on my road trips where history actually happened. I mainly stopped at National Park Service sites but also at history museums and roadside markers along with significant communities from the past. From these adventures, I wrote Driven by the Past: A Road Trip Through the American West about how the region west of the Mississippi River grew and how it changed the United States and indeed the world.
